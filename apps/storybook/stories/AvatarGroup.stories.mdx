# Avatar Group

You can group avatars together by using the `Avatar.Group` component. You can also specify a maximum number of avatars to display.

In case the elements exceed the maximum, an additional circle will be added to the right, indicating the number of additional avatars available.

---

---

## Sizes

You can also choose between `sm`, `md`, and `lg` sizes by using the `size` prop. You can also specify a custom number.

The size you choose for the group will be automatically propagated to each child.
